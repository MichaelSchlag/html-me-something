Mike's Top Animal Picks

This is the story of how there were once five dope animals that Mike decided to pick. And so, those animals are as follows:

  • Elephant
  • Bat
  • Pangolin
  • Orangutan
  • Turtle

5: Elephant

What list is complete without the elephant? Highly intelligent, unique looking, and even friendly. I wanna be pals with an elephant.

4: Bat

Bats have a bad reputation. But actually, they're very cute! I love the way they squad up in caves and old buildings.

3: Pangolin

These little dudes seem so polite. But alas, they are trafficked for their scales to make fake medicine. I just wish people would leave them alone.

2: Orangutan

I love these floppy fellas. They are very funny, especially when they swing from trees. They better not go extinct.

1: Turtles

Since early childhood, turtles have been my #1 pick. Why? Maybe their badass attitude, their unique shell, their beak, or their color. Honestly their only fault is how they smell.
